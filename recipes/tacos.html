Description

If you're looking for a little spice in your life, definitely check this recipe out.

Equal parts spicy and savoury, this taco will blow your socks off with a fiery kick!

Ingredients

  • Corn Tortillas (soft)
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Chopped White Onions
  • Skirt Steak

  • Four Pepper Salsa
    • Tomatoes
    • Diced Onions
    • Vegetable Oil
    • Green Bell Peppers
    • Poblano Peppers
    • Anaheim Chile Peppers
    • Jalapeno Pepper
    • Cilantro Leaves
    • Garlic
    • Salt

Steps

Four Pepper Salsa

  1. Rub diced tomatoes with oil and grill at medium heat for 10 minutes. Remove tomatoes from grill.
  2. Place all 4 varieties of peppers on grill for 5 minutes per side. Take care not to burn them.
  3. Let tomatoes and chile peppers cool. Peel and seed peppers and tomatoes.
  4. Put peppers, tomatoes and remaining ingredients in a food processor. Pulse until consistency is to your liking.

Tacos

  1. Marinate skirt steak in marinade of your own choice.
  2. Heat grill to high and quickly sear the steak (cooking the steak quickly on high heat is best).
  3. Cut your skirt steak into small cubes.
  4. Warm your corn tortillas. Add steak, chopped tomatoes, chopped onions and four pepper salsa onto your tortilla.
  5. Tuck your tortilla into a "U" shape and enjoy your delicious taco.
