Tomato and Egg Stir Fry

Description

Don't worry if your pantry is looking a little light, this recipe will rescue you from hunger!

This dish is super quick to whip up and will send you on a flavour rollercoaster with tangy sweet tomatoes and soft pillowy eggs.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Vegetable Oil
  • White Pepper
  • Shaoxing Wine
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Crack eggs and add salt, white pepper, shaoxing wine and sesame oil. Beat the eggs together with the seasoning.
  3. Heat pan to medium heat. Add vegetable oil to pan. Add eggs to pan until they are partially cooked and remove from pan.
  4. Add tomatoes to the pan and season with salt and sugar. If the tomatoes dry up too fast, add some water to the pan.
  5. Stir fry tomatoes for a few minutes and add the eggs back into the pan.
  6. Let tomatoes fully soften and ensure eggs are incorporated well with the tomatoes. Best served with rice.
