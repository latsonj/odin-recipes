Description

This quick and easy recipe will satisfy your carby cravings with lots of potato goodness.

Don't take this page as gospel, this dish is super versatile so don't be afraid to mix, match and make it your own!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Red Capsicums
  • Shaved Ham
  • Spring Onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika

Steps

  1. Dice potatoes, onions, red capsicums and shaved ham into bite-sized pieces and set aside.
  2. Boil water in a pot. Parboil cubed potatoes in boiling water for 5 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes and put in a pan on medium-high heat.
  4. Press down on potatoes, until one side browns and crisps. Repeat process for at least four sides until good colour and texture are distributed nicely across the potatoes.
  5. Add the rest of the diced ingredients into the pan and set to medium heat.
  6. Season with salt, pepper and smoked paprika and mix thoroughly.
  7. When ingredients reach desired colour and doneness, remove from heat. Garnish with sliced spring onion and serve.
